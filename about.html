ABOUT US

H2G IS INSPIRED BY THE VIBRANT AND VARIED URBAN ENVIRONMENTS. AS WELL AS BY STREET ART, GRAFFITI, MUSIC, BASKETBALL CULTURE AND THE LIVELY CITY LIFE. THE DESIGN CREATE A LOOK THAT SKILLFULLY COMBINES PARTS OF THE PAST AND THE PRESENT BY FUSING THEM WITH MODERN FLAIR. H2G LOOKS FOR DISTINCTIVE AND FASHIONABLE APPAREL THAT DIFFERENTIATES THEM FORM THE NORM. STREETWEAR DEVOTEES RESPECT THE ABILITY TO EXPRESS ONESELF THROUGH CLOTHING. INSTEAD OF ADHERING TO CONVENTIONAL FASHION STANDARDSS, THEY PREFER WEARING CLOTHES THAT EXPRESS THEIR PERSONALITIES AND INTEREST.
